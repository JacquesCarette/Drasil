Data Definitions

This section collects and defines all the data needed to build the instance models.

Refname DD:htTransCladFuel
Label

Effective heat transfer coefficient between clad and fuel surface

Symbol

hg
Units

\(\frac{\text{W}}{\text{m}^{2}{}^{\circ}\text{C}}\)

Equation \[{h_{\text{g}}}=\frac{2 {k_{\text{c}}} {h_{\text{p}}}}{2 {k_{\text{c}}}+{τ_{\text{c}}} {h_{\text{p}}}}\]
Description
  • hg is the effective heat transfer coefficient between clad and fuel surface (\(\frac{\text{W}}{\text{m}^{2}{}^{\circ}\text{C}}\))
  • kc is the clad conductivity (Unitless)
  • hp is the initial gap film conductance (Unitless)
  • τc is the clad thickness (Unitless)
