# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Gracefully giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Assuming good intent

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
Reports can be made by contacting the maintainers directly.
